About Me

While my home is Dallas, Texas, I frequently visit my brother in my home-away-from-home, Southern California. My journey into tech started with a little Disney movie called TRON! Fascinated with how the vernacular used in the movie translated into the design, this lead me to a career in architecture. While at my architecture job, I became the "IT Guy" troubleshooting many user issues on the spot and soon realized that my talents may lie better in another field. After taking a UX Design course, I needed to learn how to further build upon this new curiosity. Opening the door to front-end development with Skillcrsuh created a world possibilities to utilize my talents in architecture in a new and ever growing field.

When I learned how to build my first website, I realized I found something that gave me the freedom & versatility I was looking for in my work and life. Now that I've made a switch to front-end development, I can use my organization skills and eye for detail to not only design a website, but also write clean and elegant code to make the design fully functional.
